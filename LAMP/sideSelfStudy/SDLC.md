# SYSTEM DEVELOPMENT LIFE CYCLE (SDLC)
System development life cycle is the process or steps taken to create a product. It’s the stages of preparation, sorting, designing, defining, deploying and building and maintaining systems.
## STAGES OF SDLC
 1. **Planning and requirement analysis**: This stage is mainly to identify the problem, find a strategy and to get all the required resources.
 1. **Design**: System architects and designers come together in this stage and plan how the system will look like and the procedure that will be followed.
 1. **Development**: this is where all the coding and (as the name implies) the development of the system takes place following the laid down procedures that the architects have put together.
 1. **Testing and troubleshooting**: The already built system is now scrutinized, checked for errors or mistakes before it is released.
 1. **Deployment**: at this stage, the already built product is published and made available for the public. 
 1. **Maintenance**: After deployment the cycle is not near over, in fact,  it’s just the beginning to newer smaller cycles. When the public gets to use it, new ideas, errors, problems surface. *The development team then **maintains** the system: i. Plan on how to make the update or how to derive a solution to the problem that surfaced ii. Design the update or the solution framework iii. Develop the solution or the update iv. Check if the problem is solved v. Deploy.*
